About

Who's building this

Plum is built by one person — a data engineer with a background in SQL, data warehousing, and BI — working with a team of AI agents in defined roles: a data architect, an AI engineer, an adversarial evidence engineer whose mandate is to break what the others build, and a frontend developer. We say this plainly because it's true, and because it's the point: a product whose pitch is "AI you can verify" should be comfortable telling you exactly where AI sits in its own production line. Every agent's work ships only after it passes the verification gate — the same receipts-and-replay discipline the product applies to your data, applied to our own build process. The changelog is that process, in public.

Why

We analyzed the AI-commerce category in depth and found marketing claims that couldn't be reproduced — performance numbers that predated the features they advertised, demo data presented as customer results. So we built the opposite: a product where every number carries a receipt, and a website where every claim carries a command. If we ever fall short of that, the evidence page tells you how to catch us.
